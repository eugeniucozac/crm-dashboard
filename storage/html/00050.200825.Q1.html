A. SCOPE OF WORK 1

A.1: Environmental Noise Survey

We would visit the site to undertake an environmental noise survey in order to assess the current noise profile of the area. The survey would be undertaken in accordance with ISO 1996-2:2007 Acoustics “Description, measurement and assessment of environmental noise - Part 2: Determination of environmental noise levels”.

The survey would involve automated noise monitoring over a minimum period of 24 hours. All acquired data would be used to render a representative snapshot of the local background noise during daytime and night-time. Further manual measurements would also be conducted in order to assess any variations in the monitored noise levels due to specific noise sources.

During the survey, an inspection of the surrounding area would also be undertaken in order to identify the location of the nearest noise-sensitive receivers relative to the proposed plant unit location.

The acoustic descriptors LAmax, LA10, LAeq and LA90 would be measured in order to provide a more detailed understanding of the acoustic identity of the site and set noise emissions criteria, in accordance with the planning requirements of the Local Authority.
